Multiparty Quantum Computations
Goal: Compute $f(|x_1\rangle, \ldots, |x_n\rangle) =(|y_1\rangle, \ldots, |y_n\rangle)$.
Security: $P_i$ learns only $y_i$.
Denial of Service attack:
Adversary aborts the protocol.
Cannot be prevented with dishonest majority.
Nobody gets any outputs.
Quantum inputs are consumed and lost.
